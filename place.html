Kiss Bridge Phu Quoc
OVERVIEW
The Proposal Bridge has many highlights in terms of grandeur in architecture and construction, but that is not the most prominent highlight. The Proposal Bridge is the only bridge that does not connect two ends of the bridge, but in the middle of the bridge, there is a 50cm gap. This gap, on a unique occasion of the year from 24/12 to 01/01, will be filled with the image of the sun at sunset. At this moment, the bridge seems to be integrated, and couples can reach out to embrace their loved ones and exchange sweet kisses under the sunset on Pearl Island. This image, where both humans and nature are connected as one, is the symbol and the most prominent highlight that Sun Group wants to convey to every tourist when they come to the southern island.
Recently, the image of the 'Proposal Bridge' appeared in a super romantic proposal scene in the MV 'I Do' by Duc Phuc in collaboration with the band 911. The scene shows a couple dancing on the bridge in the extremely dreamy sunset light, surrounded by the beautiful and mesmerizing sky and sea. And the man kneels down to propose to the girl with a ring, the moment of love taking over sweetly like a fairy tale, touching the hearts of many young people, causing emotions to burst. Before the upcoming Valentine's Day, the Proposal Bridge appeared in a romantic and poetic way, and the MV 'I Do' immediately achieved over 1 million views after just 14 hours of being released.
From a distance, the bridge looks like a strip of silk amidst the jade blue ocean, with two parts stretching out to embrace the white sandy shore, facing each other, united as one but not touching, like the feeling of two lovers before a romantic kiss. On one end of the bridge is the Chàng restaurant, and on the other end is the Nàng restaurant, with a gap of about 50cm in the middle that does not connect them. This gap is filled only once a year from December 24th to January 1st by the image of the sunset. This is the place where Chàng and Nàng come together to exchange passionate kisses under the sunset. In the middle of the bridge, there is a space designed for family, friends from both sides to witness this memorable moment, alongside photographers who will capture this special moment. This moment where both nature and humans are united as one, is the meaning, symbol, and the most prominent highlight that the Sun Group wants to convey to every tourist visiting Phu Quoc.
