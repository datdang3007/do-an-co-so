OVERVIEW
Northwest is an area located in the northwest of Vietnam. With diverse terrain, from hills to forests and valleys of rivers and streams, the climate in the Northwest is also very diverse. In winter, the climate in the Northwest is quite cold, especially in the high mountains. Temperatures can drop below 0 degrees Celsius in some areas in winter. Meanwhile, summer in the Northwest can be hot and humid. The average temperature in summer is about 25-28 degrees Celsius. In addition, the Northwest is also an area with a lot of rain, especially in the rainy season, from May to October. Many places in this region have annual rainfall of over 2,000mm, with high mountainous areas. can be up to 4,000mm per year. This also makes the North West become one of the regions with favorable conditions for the agricultural industry
